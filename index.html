Biologist Personality Test

Find out what kind of biology career is best for you

The Biologist Personality Test was created to help aspiring scientists decide what type of science is best for them. Through a series of question, the SPT is able to give the test taker insights into their scientist personality and what types of science majors and careers would be best for them. The different types on this website can help you to decide what type of science you should persue in college, graduate school, and your career.























Developed by Professor Doug Antczak and Isabella Schell

Created by Doug Antczak of Antczak Lab at Cornell University Baker Institute for Animal Health and Isabella Schell, a research assistant at the lab. They created this test to help people learn more about what kind of biology career they should persue based off of a scientific quiz.
